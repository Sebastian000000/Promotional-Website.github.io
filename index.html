About Monarch

Monarch is a PvE server for Ark:Survival Evolved which is available on Xbox and Windows 10+. We are a small and friendly community that has been together for over 5 years and we have admins from across the word so that we can provide support to you 24/7. Monarch is a boosted cluster that includes all the arks so that you enjoy exploring and building on all the maps while still maintaining your sanity! Our boosted stats are as follows.

  • Max player level: 105(pre-ascension)
  • Max Dino level: 300
  • Harvest 5x
  • Taming 30x
  • Mature 80x
  • Hatch 40x

Player stats per point

  • Health:42
  • Stamina:30
  • Oxygen:100
  • Food:40
  • Water:40
  • Weight:7000
  • Speed:6
  • Carft:100
  • Fortitude:30

Dino stats are 1.5x officials except weights which is basically infinite

How to Join

step 1:Make sure you have the necessary DLCs downloaded

step 2:Load up Ark

step 3:Click the "join ark" button on the left side of your screen

step 4:For the easiest time ensure that Unofficial PC setting are enabled as well as having the filters on "all maps", "PvE, "ping"

step 5:In the search bar at the top type "Monarch"

step 6: Start Playing
